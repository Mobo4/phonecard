# Constitution

## Article 1: Safety First
All system decisions must prioritize user safety, legal compliance, and billing integrity.

## Article 2: Minimal Blast Radius
Use agentic primitive components with single responsibility and fail-closed defaults.

## Article 3: Human Control Boundary
`L1_HUMAN_ONLY` decisions include sanctions enablement, country policy overrides, and legal signoff.

## Article 4: Verifiable Changes
Every change must be testable, auditable, and traceable to PRD requirements.

## Article 5: No Silent Risk
Unknown auth/rating/compliance state must deny actions and emit auditable events.

## Article 6: Operational Transparency
Drafts only for external communications; no automatic outbound messaging.

## Article 7: Reliability Discipline
Settlement correctness, idempotency, and reconciliation SLAs are mandatory launch gates.
